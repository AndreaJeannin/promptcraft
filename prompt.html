Prompt

Definition: A prompt is an instruction, question, or idea used to initiate a creative response, often from a human or AI.

Use in AI: Prompts guide generative models to produce specific narrative, stylistic, or structural outcomes.

Prompt types:

  • Descriptive: “Describe a forest at night.”
  • Instructional: “Write a story where the hero fails.”
  • Seed prompt: Partial sentence or concept to expand.

Related concepts: Theme, Voice, Genre
